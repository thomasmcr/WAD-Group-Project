Exercise

Benefit of exercise

Decreased risk of various health problems such as coronary heart disease and stroke.

Increased mood, less likely to suffer from depression.

Exercise through sports can be sociable, decreases isolation

Exercises at home

Bodyweight exercises such as:

Lunges, pushups, and squats

Running, walking or biking

Group Exercise

Includes clubs such as archery, fell-walking, swimming, etc.

Has the added bonus of being highly sociable, aides in maintaining your mental health.

Clubs and groups in the local community are generally advertised in community centers, the local paper or online through social media.
