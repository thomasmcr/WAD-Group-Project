Mental Health

  • We're Mind, the mental health charity. We're here to make sure no one has to face a mental health problem alone.
  • Our goal is to make sure everyone severely affected by mental illness has a good quality of life.
  • CHUMS ethos is to support the family as a whole wherever possible and some of our services directly support adults.
  • We offer an extensive range of support services designed to help you to control your anxiety rather than letting it control you.
  • Every seven seconds, we respond to a call for help. No judgement. No pressure. We're here for anyone who needs someone.
  • If you’re struggling with mental illness, or you’re caring for someone and would like to get in touch, we’re here for you.
